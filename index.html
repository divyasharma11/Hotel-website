About Us

We are leading Riverview Hotel in Maheshwar inside The Ahilya fort and provide accommodation at the budget rates. We have rooms for every kind of people with peaceful Riverview. All the monuments are in 300 meters radius from the Hotel because you will be already in The Fort.. Spartan Hotels Group resonate true Indian Hospitality and its warmth can be felt right from the moment a guest steps in. At Shree Rajrajeshwar, we start with a traditional welcome of Namaskar and welcome our guests with a Tika, Aarti, and Garland. Spartan Hotel incorporates the Five Sense of traditional Indian welcome namely, fragrance, music, ambience, regional Indian paintings and frescoes, and mouth-watering Indian cuisines. We ensure that all the requirements of our guests are addressed on time by our professional staff, that is always on toes to provide services.
