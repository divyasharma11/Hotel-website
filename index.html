About Us

We are leading Riverview Hotel in Maheshwar inside The Ahilya fort and provide accommodation at the budget rates. At Shree Rajrajeshwar, we start with a traditional welcome of Namaskar and welcome our guests with a Tika, Aarti, and Garland. Spartan Hotel incorporates the Five Sense of traditional Indian welcome namely, fragrance, music, ambience, regional Indian paintings and frescoes, and mouth-watering Indian cuisines. We ensure that all the requirements of our guests are addressed on time by our professional staff, that is always on toes to provide services.
